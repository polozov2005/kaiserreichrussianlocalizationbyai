Re-Establishing the Rule of Law
Although we have finally freed ourselves from Republican rule, our nation's future is still in flux. The breakdown of government services has lead to many of our citizens going hungry, and riots have began to break out in the cities. We must have an emergency meeting of local leaders and higher-ups to decide how our country will be ran.
Building a Duchy
While Lombardia, once preeminent among the Italian lands, maintained some degree of independence until the War of the First Coalition, the old Duchy had been controlled by foreign powers for centuries. To establish a functional state from the carcass of the Republic of Italy, we must remind the people of their illustrious Ducal past.
Land Reform
From the Weltkrieg to the Civil War to the collapse of the Republic, we have seen our fair share of turmoil and war. Although we would all like to move forward, it is a sad fact that our fields and farmers are simply not prepared to sustain our country after being ravaged by two decades of war and unrest. We must begin land reform processes to ensure our people have enough to eat.
Agricultural Subsidies
Although our initial land reform efforts have been successful, it will still take time before farmers are fully accustomed to the new practises. In the meantime, we should issue subsidies to farmers and agricultural workers to ensure they are able to keep up with demand.
Wounds of War
After 20 years of war, the many wounds gouged into Lombardy and her people are finally beginning to recover. However, if we truly wish to mend this trauma, a comprehensive societal and governmental effort will be required- one that our new Duke can be the centre of.
The Duchy's Constitution
Our Duchy is young and its people uncertain of how the new government will rule them. With the serious issues plaguing our nation dealt with, the time has come to draft our new constitution and finalise the structure of our government.
A Duchy of the Lombards
While the Duke is undoubtedly virtuous and just, he and the Lombard people alike understand the value of representative government. Absolute rule will no longer be tolerated by the people, and the time has finally come for Lombards to decide their own future.
Strengthen the Duke
Lombardia's righteous Duke is like a father to the people. Like any good father, he must at times be firm with his children and guide their development. If justice and good governance are to ever be assured, the Duke must be the ultimate authority in Lombadia.
Denounce the Golden Ambrosian Idea
With the restoration of Lombardia, many Lombard Republicans, undeterred by the failure of their beloved Italian Republic, embraced the legacy of old Ambrosia, a uniquely Lombard strain of Republicanism. We must once and for all repudiate republicanism while also affirming constitutional governance.
Request Return of the Lombard Crown
The Iron Crown of Lombardy, inlaid with a Nail of the True Cross, was the centrepiece of the coronation of the old Frankish kings of Italy and later rested upon the heads of Holy Roman Emperors. It was taken by Austria following its victory in the Weltkrieg; with Lombardia no longer in total disrepair, the time has come to request the return of this important symbol of legitimacy and Lombardia itself.
Crown the King of the Lombards
With the Iron Crown of Lombardy in our possession and our nation firmly established, the good Duke no longer need be sullied by a title unworthy of a man of such virtue. For his sake and our people's, let us proclaim the Kingdom of [ITA.GetNonIdeologyName].
A Lombard Republic
Although we are the remnants of the Republic of Italy, we must stop thinking of ourselves as Italians. Our future lies in being Lombardy and in reviving the consciousness of the Lombard people. The new Lombard Republic begins here!
A Question of Radicals
Within our nation there are certain circles that aim for a more... radical form of governance, the largest of them being the Lega Longobarda. We must grapple with the question of if we should ban these radical groups to protect our young democracy, or if we must let them grow in the name of free speech and liberty.
The Republican Constitution
The Lombard people are desperate for a return to normalcy, and it is our duty to provide it to them. The time has come to write a new constitution, one which protects our Republican ideals and enshrines the rights of the Lombard people.
Secure Vital Industries
The future of our country depends not on the farmer, but on the factory worker. We must encourage industry within our country and pass corporation-friendly legislature to ensure a strong industrial base.
Heart of the North
Although our country is small, the Po valley we are situated in has long been the industrial centre of Italy. We must do everything in our power to resurrect the working spirit of our people and grow our industry far beyond what our population would suggest is possible.
The Golden Ambrosia Idea
The Golden Ambrosia Republic was a brief period where Milan existed as a republic, free of the burdens of aristocracy. This uniquely Langobard idea must be revived- only once the nobility has been driven out of Lombardy shall our people truly be free.
A Republican Nobility
The aristocracy remains a powerful force in Lombardia, and we cannot entertain the idea of driving them out. We should establish a House of Lords similar to the one in the former United Kingdom to ensure noble voices are heard.
Stamp Out Monarchism
Monarchism is an outdated institution and has no place in the 20th century. The Republic of Italy may have collapsed, but the republican values it espoused shall live on!
Lombardy's Place in the World
As a newly founded nation, many of the more well-established countries of the world are wary to lend their support to us. We must show the world that we have as much a place as any other nation, and forge new alliances.
Fortress Milan
As one of the largest cities in the whole of the Italian Peninsula, special measures must be taken to ensure the safety of Milan. Fortunately, the newly-founded nobility of Lombardy agrees with us on this, and has generously decided to invest in arms manufacturing in and around the city.
The Republic Triumphant
Despite the odds being stacked against us in nearly every way, our Republic has conquered the challenges it faced and has rose from the ashes of the Weltkrieg and Civil War to become a nation as well-respected as any other!
Extinguish the Flames of Republicanism
Wherever one looks, they see the failures of republicanism. The Italian Republic lies shattered, Russia is a shambling shell of the great power it once was, and even the once-mighty United States lies teetering on the edge of civil war. We must crush any remaining republican movements in our country, lest we end up like them.
Encourage Lombard Nationalism
The Lombard people have witnessed defeat after defeat and suffered countless tragedy in recent years. Their spirit and love of their homeland, however, has not been broken. We must harness this unyielding quality of character; let every man be emboldened to defend his home!
Youth Lombard League
In the hearts of its youth is a nation's integrity reinforced or destroyed. To build a stronger nation, the young of Lombardia must be instilled with national pride and an unwavering moral rectitude. A national organisation for the promotion of such qualities should be established immediately.
Stengthen the Border
The syndicalists to the south pose a grave danger to the existence of Lombardy, vastly outnumbering us both in population and industry. If Lombardy is to survive the upcoming war, we must invest into an extensive line of forts along the Po River.
Economic Reorganisation
Lombardia's economy was devastated by the Weltkrieg and ravages of the socialists, and has yet to fully recover. To remedy this, nearly every aspect of the economy must eventually be reorganised.
Repair Efforts
During the Weltkrieg and Civil War, and later the turmoil caused by the collapse of the Republic of Italy, much of our industry was damaged or destroyed. We must invest in repairing damaged industry if we ever want our economy to recover.
Expand Milanese Industrial Capacity
Milan, Lombardia's capital and industrial heart, due to its proximity to the Ticino River, sustained significant damage in the Italian Civil War. Now, this great city may at last be rebuilt and its industry expanded.
Expand Brescia Industrial Capacity
Brescia, while an important centre of culture, has long lagged behind the much of northern Italy in industrial development. Industrial expansion in Brescia can only be beneficial to the city and all Lombardia.
Re-Armament Plans
Lombardia was an important centre of armaments production prior to the Weltkrieg, and with investment, it can be again.
Lombard Heavy Industry
Lombardia has long supported a vibrant array of heavy industry, and with the country slowly recovering, through government investment heavy industry can thrive again.
New Railways
Many railways lie abandoned, and as Lombardia's economy has shifted, some are no longer worth rebuilding. As a result, new railways are needed to connect our industrial centres.
Raw Material Investments
Lombard steel production was severely affected by conflict due to the complete cessation of iron mining operations throughout the region. We should renovate the mines and invest in steel production.
Education Reform
The threat of radicalism can only be fully ended if our nation's youth have the fortitude to reject its corruption. Our schools must instil values and represent political ideologies that do not work contrary to our nation's interests.
The University of Milan
Milan's university has been a shining centre of learning for hundreds of years. The time has come to fully promote it and incorporate its research into a broader national system.
Lombard Renaissance

Patron of the Arts and Sports
Our leader [LOM.GetLeader] is truly a Renaissance Man, and among his many passions are the arts and the art of sport. Inspired by his example, many of Lombardia's wealthiest citizens have also donated significantly to the arts and sporting organisations. We should give further state support to these areas and ensure the contentment of our people.
An Industrious People
Throughout history, Lombardia has been an economic powerhouse. With some encouragement and government investment, there is no reason why we cannot be one today as well.
Longobardian Revivalism
The Longobards, for whom Lombardia is named, were a Germanic people who conquered much of Italy in the sixth century. With our cultural Renaissance, many in our nation have become increasingly interested in them. We should promote this revival of interest in the Longobards to further solidify our national identity.
Re-Establishing the Army of the Lombards
Our young nation has many old Italian divisions, it is true, but they are not loyal to the Lombard state that we are now. We must work to create a capable domestic military free of Italian influence if we want to survive against the more powerful Italian states.
Gian Galleazzo Officer School
Much like Gian Galleazzo, the first Duke of Milan, had a dream of uniting Northern Italy and reviving the Lombard Empire, we dream of one day seeing a reunited Italy. In honour of him and his dream, we will name an officer school after him from which the next generation of Lombard commanders shall be trained.
Equipment Purchases
It pains us to look for our arms from elsewhere, but Lombardia currently does not have the capacity to manufacture a great deal of its own weaponry. Until this changes, we should look to purchase foreign arms and equipment.
Heavy Armaments Industry
Heavy mechanical equipment is the backbone of the modern army, and we are severely lacking in it. We should capitalise on our industrial capacity to promote the growth a domestic industry for artillery and other heavy armaments.
Recruitment Efforts
While modern warfare is defined by of machines and industry, an army cannot function without men. We should capitalise on the scores of young men inspired by our efforts to revive Lombard nationalism to pad out our army.
Drill the Troops into Shape!
Our troops lack experience, and while training is no replacement for actual combat, to make up for the small size of our armed forces, our green units must be as prepared for war as is possible. To accomplish this, we must hire new drill instructors.
A Lombard Heavy Armour Brigade
Cavalry, the age-old lynchpin of tactical success, is increasingly being supplanted by armoured vehicles. If we are to succeed on the battlefields of today, we must have tanks.
Artillery Purchases
While our investment in the heavy armaments industry has alleviated some of our shortages of heavy equipment, demand for artillery pieces still remains largely unaffected. We should continue our policy of foreign weapon procurement, this time looking to purchase artillery.
Combat Doctrines
With our military now largely established and new drill instructors hired, we can further develop our military doctrine.
Armaments Above All
We shall never have the manpower to fight a prolonged conflict with the great powers of the world, so our nation's industry must become the lynchpin of our national defence. By producing large numbers of heavy armaments used effectively in the field, our soldiers can inflict disproportionate casualties disproportionate to their number.
Outsmart the Enemy
